Music

Baile Funk · Global Bass · Club Music

Don Low is an internationally renowned DJ and producer. His unique style, blending powerful Baile Funk with Bass and ethnic influences has captivated crowds at hundreds of venues around the world — Latin America, Asia, Europe, India, North Africa.

He has collaborated with Klap, Smoothies, DJ Smilez and Caïo Prince. His tracks have resonated in Boiler Rooms and clubs worldwide, supported by Nas Thug, Klap, Klean, Tropkillaz, Tye Turner and Baby J — appearing on National TV and official NBA games.

Founder of DON. Records — a key player in both the Funk and Bass music scenes.
